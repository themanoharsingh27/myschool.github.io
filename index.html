about us

Smt. V. M. English Medium was established in 1987-88 by Shri. Halol Mahajan Uchcha Shikshan Mandal, with a view to impart sound education to all the children irrespective of caste, creed or colour and to prepare them to live and act as idle citizens of India. The Mandal also aims at developing the student’s character, personality and national consciousness. The school is recognized by the Department of Education of the State of Gujarat admission is open to all the communities per the norms laid down. The school has gained the momentum and has impressed very well upon the parents. Smt. V. M. English Medium School has now completed 25 years with a strength of more than 1500 students at present. It stands as a full fledged school with the Pre-primary, Primary, Secondary and Higher Secondary sections both in Science and Commerce Stream. The school has presented number of batches in SSC, HSC in Science and Commerce Stream. All the batches achieved hundred percent results which adds a colourful feather to our achievements. Our school pays much attention to the pupils to raise up their standard in idles and attainments and hence along with the usual education, we have introduced Computer Labs, Music Room, Science Laboratory for various subjects and a well Equipped Library as well. The school also encourages the students to participate and win in the Inter-School competitions of various Curricular & Co-Curricular activities with a view to develop their all around personality right from the beginning of their educational career. Over all to sum up the President and the members of the committee along with the Principal – Staff members and parents deserve congratulation for their earnest co-operation in the growth of the school.
